# gw2-reffect-boosterspack

A reffect pack for better presentation of all kinds of booster effects

![image](documentation/image.png)

Buffs are presented under 5 categories:

- General boosters
- Guild related buffs: Banners & Guild Bonuses
- Fractal Boosters: Potions & God Achievement
- Food/Util
- Jade Buffs

## Notes

- I had to use some AI made icons to represent guild buffs. And they are ugly. I plan to make a fix at... some point

- Since some foods/utils have the same EffectID, I had to choose. So you may see the image of "my choice" instead of what you actually consumed
